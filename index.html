MODIS

Architectural Models

MODIS is an architectural modeling company created by Nashville based architect Tommy Brown with the belief that a physical model is the most effective means of design communication. Tommy has a Masters of Architecture degree from the University of Tennessee, and has 15 years experience making physical models for award winning design firms such as Tuck-Hinton Architects, Carney Architects, and L.P. Brown and Associates. If you are interested in having a model built by MODIS, please contact us.
